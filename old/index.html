Welcome! ¡Bienvenidos!

I am an atmospheric scientist studying high-impact weather (especially tropical cyclones/hurricanes/typhoons) with a combination of numerical models and observations. Currently, I work at the National Center for Atmospheric Research as an Advanced Study Program Postdoctoral Fellow. I got my PhD from the University at Albany, where I worked with Ryan Torn on tropical cyclone intensification under moderate vertical wind shear. In this website you will find information about my research, including lists and links to my publications and presentations. You will also find a list of scholarships, internships, and other opportunities for atmospheric science students.
